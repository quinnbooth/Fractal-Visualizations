Barnsley Fern
The Barnsley Fern and its variants are fractals generated via probabilistic matrix operations. A point is plotted and then its coordinates are transformed using one of the four operations below: multiplication by matrix [[A, B], [C, D]], followed by addition with [[E], [F]]. This repeats until a fern-like image appears.
